About Us

Transformation
Through Education

The principal reason we continue to adapt and evolve our business model is to ensure that we are meeting our customers expectation. It originated from the idea that there exists a class of readers who respond better to online content and prefer to learn new skills at their own pace from the comforts of their drawing rooms. Every individual has the potential to create change, whether in their life, their community, or the world. The transformative power of education is what unlocks that potential. Yet, access to high-quality education has been a privilege of the few. So, we realized it was a time for a seismic shift in learning. From the tried and true to the leading edge. From “for some” to “for all.” By opening the classroom through online learning, Electron empowers millions of learners to unlock their potential and become changemakers.



Mission

Our mission is to deliver Simply Easy Learning with clear, crisp, and to-the-point content on a electronics subjects without any preconditions and impediments.
